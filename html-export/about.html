About Xinhang

Purposeful, global, and quietly ambitious.

Xinhang is designed for families who want more than a school schedule: rigorous academics, steady mentorship, residential culture, and a pathway that prepares students to step confidently into the wider world.

School Character

An international school with boarding-school polish.

The school experience is intentionally structured: students learn in classrooms, practice independence in daily life, and build confidence through ceremony, public expression, athletics, and service.

Its identity is rooted in Jinan and open to the world. The result should feel calm, selective, and serious: a campus where students are known, challenged, and expected to grow.

Our Approach

Growth is not left to chance.

Academic planning, boarding routines, faculty guidance, and student activities work as one system. Each student is expected to develop habits of preparation, expression, collaboration, and reflection.

Mentorship
Faculty relationships that help students set goals and follow through.
Belonging
A school culture built through shared routines, houses, ceremonies, and teams.
Standards
Clear expectations for academic effort, conduct, and personal responsibility.

School Philosophy

Make Each Life Bloom Wonderfully.

The school motto is treated as a practical promise: to notice each student clearly, to create room for excellence, and to help different forms of talent become visible.
