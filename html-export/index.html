About Xinhang

An international school with boarding-school polish.

Xinhang serves families who value serious academics, residential growth, and an international outlook. Classroom expectations, daily routines, college planning, and public expression are placed on one coherent path of development.

Education here is not only about results. It also values how students learn, work with others, and express themselves when the moment matters. A stable campus culture makes effort visible.
